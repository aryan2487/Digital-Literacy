Do's:

Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review and restrict who can see your personal information and posts.

Think Before You Post: Consider if a future employer or professor would find your content professional.

Verify Information: Fact-check news or academic updates before sharing them to prevent the spread of misinformation.

Network Professionally: Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with alumni, industry experts, and peers.

Support Your Peers: Use your digital presence to share helpful resources, study tips, and positive campus news.

Don'ts:

Post Sensitive Data: Never share your home address, phone number, or academic ID numbers publicly.

Engage with Trolls: Avoid getting into heated public arguments or "flame wars" that can damage your digital reputation.

Share Illegal Content: Do not post or distribute copyrighted materials, pirated software, or prohibited content.

Plagiarize Work: Never claim someone else’s digital content, designs, or code as your own without proper credit.

Post During Class: Avoid using social media during lectures unless it is part of a sanctioned academic activity.S
